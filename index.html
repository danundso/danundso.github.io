Below is the change detection project I worked on for remote sensing. I met with Ukrainians to look at the change of the land cover in the Danube Delta region, especially the change in hydrology over time

We decided to compare two Landsat images, one from 1984 (on the left) and one from 2020 (on the right) based on training data Rewilding Europe gave us.




We also looked at how the reintroduction of water buffalo on Ermakov Island in Ukraine changed the vegetation of the island over time. We used the same two Landsat images, one from 1984 (on the left) and one from 2020 (on the right) and the same training data from Rewilding Europe.
